= TAKE DEBIT PAYMENTS EASILY RIGHT ON YOUR WEBSITE =

Cevnn's WooCommerce plug-in allows you to take debit payments directly on your store via the Cevnn API.

Cevnn is available in Canada and coming soon to the U.S.

= WHY CHOOSE CEVNN? =
Cevnn has no setup fees, no monthly fees, no hidden costs: you only get charged when you earn money! Earnings are transferred to your bank account on a 2-day rolling basis so you get your funds quick!
